# Google Play Store Image Requirements

## App Icon
- **Size:** 512 x 512 px
- **Format:** PNG (32-bit)
- **Max file size:** 1 MB
- **Required:** Yes
- **Note:** Must be a full square, no transparency

---

## Feature Graphic
- **Size:** 1024 x 500 px
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Max file size:** 1 MB
- **Required:** Yes
- **Usage:** Displayed at top of store listing

---

## Phone Screenshots
- **Minimum required:** 2 screenshots
- **Maximum allowed:** 8 screenshots
- **Dimensions:** 
  - Min: 320 px on shortest side
  - Max: 3840 px on longest side
  - Aspect ratio: Between 16:9 and 9:16
- **Recommended:** 1080 x 1920 px or 1080 x 2340 px (portrait)
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Max file size:** 8 MB each
- **Required:** Yes

---

## 7-inch Tablet Screenshots
- **Minimum required:** 2 screenshots
- **Maximum allowed:** 8 screenshots
- **Recommended dimensions:** 1200 x 1920 px
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Max file size:** 8 MB each
- **Required:** Yes (MANDATORY)

---

## 10-inch Tablet Screenshots
- **Minimum required:** 2 screenshots
- **Maximum allowed:** 8 screenshots
- **Recommended dimensions:** 1600 x 2560 px
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Max file size:** 8 MB each
- **Required:** Yes (MANDATORY)

---

## Promo Video (Optional)
- **Format:** YouTube URL only
- **Required:** No
- **Note:** No file upload, just provide YouTube link

---

## Screenshot Requirements

### All Screenshots Must:
- ✅ Not include device frames (phones, tablets, etc.)
- ✅ Show actual app content
- ✅ Be in the same orientation (all portrait or all landscape)
- ✅ Not contain ads or inappropriate content
- ✅ Be high-quality and representative of actual app experience
- ✅ Min 320 px on shortest side, max 3840 px on longest side
- ✅ Aspect ratio between 16:9 and 9:16

### Best Practices:
- Use actual screenshots from your app
- Keep text readable and clear
- Show key features and functionality
- First 2-3 screenshots are most important (appear above the fold)
- Use consistent visual style across all screenshots
- Highlight unique selling points
